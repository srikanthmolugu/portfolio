TOURIST PLACES

1. Chadar - The Frozen River Trek

How often does one boast about walking on a frozen river in a dreamlike destination? Stamped as one of the most thrilling experiences in Ladakh for an adventure aficionado, the Chadar trek route connects villages in the Zanskar valley deep in the mountains with Chilling and the frozen Zanskar River.

2. Manali-Leh Road Trip

The 'Mecca' of all road trips in India, the Manali-Leh Highway has always been a favourite. Be it for the groups or solo travellers, this route outshines any other backpacking trips in India. All -you need to do: get geared up for some adventure, hit the road and reach nowhere! Passing mountains and villages, passing people who are going about their business in a more simple way what will get you appreciate both nature and simplicity.

3.Pushkar

Regarded as one of the oldest city in India which consist of interesting leftovers of a past time, Pushkar is a must visit for you. Make your getaway more satisfying as you investigate some of the most interesting places here.This little destination brags lot of places to enjoy, best in class resorts and has the Brahma Temple, which is one of its kind in the whole world. Whether you are visiting this place to learn about the ancient culture or just for some recess time, this place will never let you down. And guys, don't forget to visit the famous fair which is very eagerly celebrated here.

4.Sunderbans

The Sundarbans National Park is the most recommended place to be visited with your best friend. It has been declared as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. This National Park encompasses a long stretch across India and Bangladesh, that includes around 54 islands.The Sundarbans National Park is an abode to the largest number of wild tigers. According to the sources, there are approximately 400 tigers here.There are a variety of Other wild species that dwell here like the rock pythons, water monitors, monkey and chital.

5.Meghalaya

The land of mesmerizing scenic beauty formed of high hills, undulating streams and waterfalls. Also known as the ‘Abode of Clouds’, you will never cease to wonder how you could have missed this place. What’s more, on the off chance that you are with you best friend then this place is a must visit for you. Isolating Assam from Bangladesh this majestic land is known for its rocky cliffs and its mysterious caves have so many secrets within. And yes if you adore the rains then this is you call. You will find Cherrapunji and Mawsyram two of the wettest land on earth. Folks, you will stay dumbstruck as you can witness the snow white mists going through the mountains gradually playing with you. Capture each and every moment with your friend while you visit this place.

6.Gokarna

There is no doubt in that beaches are like the heaven on earth for those who love spending time in the waters. This small temple town has become of the best tourist places in India which is visited all-round the year. With the lively markets and the places to visit this place has lot to offer. Bask in the sun in the beaches or just head to the temple you will find this place an interesting one with.

7.Munnar

There is always a part of us which is waiting for an adventure. Imagine a place where you not just enjoy the cool weather but also try some heart wrenching activities. Munnar is one of the best places to visit in India. Known for the mountains ranges covered by a by a vast greenery and away from the hustle and bustle of city life this place is the prefect treat for spending some time with nature.

Sakleshpur

What could to be better than waking up to morning with the most glorious sunlight and sipping a hot cup of coffee? Sakleshpur is one such place which is must visit for you. This is also one of the best tourist destination in India.Soak in a bit of nature in the misty clouds as you visit this exciting place. Lose yourself as you witness the milky waters cascading down the hills and submerging in the gentle water provides a kaleidoscopic caparison. So while you are planning a getaway this place should be at the top of your bucket list.
